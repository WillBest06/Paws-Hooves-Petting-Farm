Welcome to Paws & Hooves Petting farm

  • With a small group of friendly animals and countless amazing plants across 13 acres of beautiful, sub-tropical gardens, you’re in for a fantastic day out at Paws & Hooves Petting Zoo!
  • Located in Cornwall, Paws & Hooves Petting Zoo is a brilliant day out for all the family. It’s a place to roam, discover, explore and get in touch with nature.
  • We’re a registered charity and support a number of projects in the UK and overseas that aim to help halt species decline.
